Frequently asked

What is IntoDay?
IntoDay is a lightweight day planner for the things you need to do today — without needing to assign exact times.
How is this different from a calendar?
Calendars are for exact events. IntoDay is for the things you want to place into your day without turning them into calendar entries.
Do I need to set exact times?
No. IntoDay is built around time periods like morning, afternoon, and evening — so you can stay organized without planning everything to the minute.
Can I move things later?
Yes. If plans change, you can move items to a later time period or another day without starting over.
Is this a to-do list app?
Not exactly. IntoDay gives your to-dos a place in the day, so they feel more organized than a simple list.
Who is IntoDay for?
It’s for anyone managing follow-ups, reminders, errands, and small plans that don’t need exact times.
