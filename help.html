Frequently asked

What is IntoDay?
IntoDay is a minimal day-planning application designed to bring intention and clarity to your daily routine, without the overwhelming clutter of traditional project management tools.
How is this different from a calendar?
While calendars track hard appointments, IntoDay focuses on the rhythm of your tasks and intentions. It's about how you want to feel and what you want to focus on, not just where you need to be.
Do I need to set exact times?
Not at all. We encourage time-blocking by broader phases of the day (morning, afternoon, evening) to allow for flexibility and flow, rather than rigid minute-by-minute scheduling.
Can I move things later?
Yes. Life happens. You can easily drag and drop items to the next day or a future backlog without feeling penalized by the system.
